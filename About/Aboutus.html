At first, we thought just to create a website that would help people learn how to draw, despite their age. The site was used by only a few people, and even then rarely. And often added changes.

Then the site began to rapidly gain popularity. And we had to spend more time on the site. Little by little, we began to grow towards the top.

By 2017, we had officially opened a company to teach drawing at any level. We have successfully completed almost 4 years of training of 40,000 students in our 50 branches in the CIS. During this time, we have created dozens of new long-term and short-term educational programs, and launched an online direction.

In the future

And in the future, we plan to open dozens more branches abroad.


About the project



Suret School is a network of educational centers operating in 5 countries and more than 50 cities.

Since 2017, more than 40,000 students have been enrolled in our training centers. In our schools, you can get training in 20 specialties in the field of design, media and programming. Each course is a squeeze of applied knowledge from more than 200 expert practitioners who have worked to create our programs. The training takes place in two formats: online, in the form of live broadcasts with feedback and homework, and traditional-directly in your city.
